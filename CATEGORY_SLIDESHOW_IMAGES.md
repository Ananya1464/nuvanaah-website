## Technical Specifications

### File Naming Convention:
```
category-slideshow-{category-name}.png
```
Examples:
- `category-slideshow-welcome.png`
- `category-slideshow-mastectomy.png`
- `category-slideshow-chemo.png`
- `category-slideshow-recovery.png`
- `category-slideshow-lymphedema.png`
- `category-slideshow-hairloss.png`

### Storage Location:
```
d:\nuvaanah\public\images\categories\
```

### Optimization:
- **Max file size:** 80KB per image
- **Recommended:** Use TinyPNG or similar compression
- **Format preference:** WebP for web, PNG fallback
- **Alt text:** Descriptive for accessibility

---

## Design Guidelines

### Brand Consistency:
- Use Nuvanaah color palette (teal, cream, sage)
- Maintain dignity and warmth in all imagery
- Avoid overly clinical or sterile aesthetics
- Focus on hope, comfort, and quality

### Photography Style:
- **Lighting:** Soft, natural, warm
- **Background:** Clean, uncluttered (cream/white/sage gradients)
- **Angle:** Straight-on or slight angle (not extreme)
- **Mood:** Reassuring, professional, compassionate

### Avoid:
- Stock photos of patients (privacy/dignity concerns)
- Medical facility backgrounds
- Cold/clinical color grading
- Busy or distracting backgrounds
