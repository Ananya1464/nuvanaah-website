## GLOBAL STYLE GUIDELINES

**For ALL Product Images:**

1. **Lighting:** Soft, natural, warm - avoid harsh shadows
2. **Colors:** Incorporate brand colors (teal, sage, cream, gold) subtly
3. **Background:** Clean, minimal - white, cream, or soft sage
4. **Mood:** Dignified, warm, trustworthy, premium quality
5. **Avoid:** Clinical/hospital aesthetic, overly medical appearance
6. **Resolution:** High-resolution, e-commerce ready (minimum 2000px width)
7. **Format:** JPG, optimized for web
8. **Aspect Ratio:** 4:3 or 1:1 for consistency

**Photography Style:**
- Premium e-commerce product photography
- Lifestyle images should feel warm and hopeful
- Detail shots should showcase quality and craftsmanship
- Usage images should be educational but respectful

**Brand Voice Through Imagery:**
- Confidence and dignity
- Luxury and quality
- Care and support
- Trust and professionalism
